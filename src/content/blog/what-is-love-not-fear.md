Every decision you make comes from one of two places.

Love or fear.

The job you took. The conversation you avoided. The goal you set. The one you didn't. When you look at any choice through this lens, you start seeing a pattern. And once you see it, you can't unsee it.

That's the core of Love Not Fear. Not a theory. A diagnostic tool that makes the invisible visible.

## The 8-Week Program

The program runs in small cohorts of 6 people, meeting once a week for an hour with homework between sessions. Here's what you work through:

**Week 1: The Diagnostic**
You start with the Three-Two-One exercise: 3 events that shaped you, 2 people who influenced you, 1 decision that defined your path. This surfaces patterns you've been running on autopilot.

**Week 2: Know Your Wiring**
Personality and mindset testing. You can't lead yourself if you don't know how you're built. We use psychometric tools to map your natural tendencies, strengths, and blind spots.

**Week 3: Core Values**
Most people can't name their top 5 values. This week, you identify them. Not the aspirational ones. The real ones driving your actual behavior.

**Week 4: Vision and Mission**
With your values clear, you build a personal vision and mission. Not a corporate mission statement. A declaration of what your life is actually for.

**Week 5: 90-Day Plan**
Vision without execution is a wish. You take your mission and break it into a 90-day plan across three dimensions: Personal, Professional, and People.

**Week 6: Habits and Systems**
This is where it gets operational. You design daily habits that keep the system running without relying on motivation or discipline. Ivan put it perfectly: "Don't rely on discipline. You will not be disciplined all the time, but you'll always have a habit that will remind you."

**Week 7: Immunity to Change**
You identify the hidden commitments that sabotage your stated goals. This is the exercise that makes people uncomfortable. That's how you know it's working.

**Week 8: Vision Board**
You create a visual representation of the life you're building. Not a Pinterest board. A deeply personal image that your nervous system can aim at every morning.

## The Three Dimensions

Everything in the program maps to three life areas:

→ **Personal** — Mind, body, spirit. Your relationship with yourself.
→ **Professional** — Career, impact, contribution. What you build.
→ **People** — Family, relationships, community. Who you build it with.

Most people over-index on one and neglect the others. The program forces balance across all three.

## Why "Love Not Fear"?

Fear isn't always obvious. It doesn't always look like panic or paralysis. Sometimes it looks like perfectionism. Sometimes it looks like overwork. Sometimes it looks like saying yes to everything because saying no feels risky.

Love isn't always soft. Sometimes love is the hard conversation. Sometimes it's setting a boundary. Sometimes it's leaving something that isn't working.

The program teaches you to recognize which operating system is running — love or fear — and gives you the tools to switch deliberately. Not once. Every day. Through habits, not willpower.

As Ivan said: "When shit hits the fan, you know what needs to be done. Instead of having the discipline, you'll always have a habit that will remind you."
